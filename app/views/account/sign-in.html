Sign in to check the Children’s Barred List

{#

Use this service to check if an individual appears on the Children’s Barred List.

#}

You can use this service if you’re either:

  • hiring a new employee and you’re waiting for the result of an Enhanced DBS
  • hiring an existing employee who has worked in a school or college where they had regular contact with children
{# Schools, colleges, local authorities and universities can use this search to check the children's barred list status of individuals who they are considering engaging in regulated activity. This search should only be used to check the barred list status of a new starter pending the receipt of an Enhanced DBS or where an individual has worked in post in a school or college that brought them into regular contact with children or young persons which ended not more than three months prior to that person's appointment to the organisation. If a potential match is returned please contact DBS via email dbscost@dbs.gov.uk.
